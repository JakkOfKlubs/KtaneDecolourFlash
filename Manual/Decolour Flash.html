On the Subject of Decolour Flash

Normally I would spell the C word as color, but because every other module of this kind has the same spelling, I am forced to spell the C word as colour. I’m sorry.

The screen will flash a sequence of ten word-colour pairs. Only press YES if the sequence is in a LEGAL state. Only press NO if the sequence is in an ILLEGAL state.

The sequence is in an ILLEGAL state if any of these statements are true:

  • There is GREEN word in a RED colour.
  • A prime number of pairs in this sequence have matching words and colours.
  • A MAGENTA word is followed by a RED, GREEN, or BLUE, colour.
  • Any three pairs in a row contain the same colour as its word or colour, unless this colour is not present anywhere in the previous sequence.
  • A YELLOW word has a VIOLET word before it in the sequence.
  • A CYAN word is present in the sequence, unless the previous stage had an ORANGE colour.
  • Four words in a row do not contain a blue component, or five colours in a row do not contain a blue component.
  • There is a VIOLET word in either a JADE colour or ORANGE colour.
  • An AZURE word is present in the first or second pairs of the sequence, unless a YELLOW colour is present in the sequence.
  • There is exactly one LIME colour or word or exactly one ORANGE colour or word, but not both.
  • The first word is equal to the last colour, or the first colour is equal to the last word, unless either of them are ROSE.

Upon six NO presses, the module will automatically disarm.
